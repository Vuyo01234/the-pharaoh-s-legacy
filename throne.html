The Golden Audience Hall

An immersive environment design portraying the grand throne room of Kemet. Modeled in rigid symmetrical perspective, the space projects absolute cosmic stability and the divine order of Ma'at.

Architectural Elements

  • Symmetrical Columns: Tall papyriform columns carved with sacred cartouches flank the grand walkway, establishing depth and height.
  • The Solar Hearth: Placed above the throne, reflecting the golden solar rays of Ra onto the Pharaoh's seating dais.
  • Hieroglyphic Reliefs: Walls detailed with recessed frescoes documenting historical victories and divine offerings.
  • Sacred Offerings: Royal urns, incense burners, and carved gold statuary arranged symmetrically around the royal court.

Visual Theme

Constructed with a deep charcoal background juxtaposed against a radiant, warm golden sheen, creating high-contrast celestial illumination.
